Courses Available

Each course runs for 6-12 weeks, combining classroom learning with practical assignments and field experience.

Photography Mastery

Manual camera settings, lighting, posing, editing, studio & outdoor mastery with live projects.

Videography & Editing

Scripting, shot composition, audio, Premiere Pro/DaVinci workflows, colour grading and storytelling.

Makeup Artistry

Beauty fundamentals, editorial looks, bridal makeup, male grooming and on-set protocols.

Graphics & Branding

Photoshop, Illustrator, brand identity design, flex and flyer production, social media creatives.

Livestream & Media Tech

Switcher operations, camera routing, audio mixing, internet backup strategies and hybrid events.

Creative Business

Pricing, client onboarding, contracts, sales funnels, branding and growth strategies for creatives.
