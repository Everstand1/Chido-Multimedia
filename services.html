Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the common questions we receive from clients.

How early should I book Chido Multimedia LTD?

We recommend booking 4-8 weeks ahead for major events, and at least 7 days in advance for studio or passport sessions. Rush bookings are available subject to schedule.

Do you travel outside Abuja and Ebonyi?

Yes. Our crew is available for projects across Nigeria with logistics coordinated in advance. Travel and accommodation are billed separately.

Can we customise our package?

Absolutely. Every package can be tailored to include makeup, livestream, drone, additional photographers, highlight reels, photobooks and more.

What is your delivery timeline?

Preview photos are delivered within 48-72 hours for most events while complete edits arrive within 1-3 weeks depending on your package.
