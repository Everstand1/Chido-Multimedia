Our Origin Story

From a Single Camera to a Nationwide Creative Collective

Chido Multimedia LTD started over a decade ago with a single camera, a strong work ethic and a dedication to excellence. From covering weddings in Abuja to training photographers across Nigeria, we have grown into a trusted multimedia house with a reputation for prompt delivery and premium production values.

Every project we take on is handled with the care and innovation it deserves. We combine human empathy with advanced technology to bring concepts alive.

Our Promise

We are committed to creating purposeful visuals that reflect the dignity, joy and beauty of our clients’ stories.

  • Collaborative project planning
  • State-of-the-art equipment and studios
  • Experienced directors, photographers & editors
  • Dedicated client support team

Milestones & Impact

Consistent innovation and relationships have allowed us to expand our reach from Lugbe to the rest of Nigeria.

2013 · The Beginning

Chido Multimedia launched with wedding photography, quickly becoming a recommended brand in Abuja.

2016 · Full-Service Studio

Opened our Lugbe Zone 5 studio, added makeup, indoor set design and graphics department.

2019 · Nationwide Expansion

Introduced livestreaming, drone coverage and on-location mobile units serving clients across Nigeria.

2022 · Training Academy Launch

Started the Chido Creative Academy, graduating over 400 students in photography, videography and makeup artistry.
